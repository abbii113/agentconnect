# Competitor brief — Realiste.ai (now "Titleman")

## What they are
AI real-estate platform for agents/investors, UAE-first, going global. Two angles:
- **For agents** (realiste.ai/en/for-agents): a free, white-label AI platform — *"Earn up to 90% of the
  developer's commission. Keep your clients, get paid reliably, find properties 10× faster."*
- **Homepage** has pivoted to a **white-label "National Platform Builder"** ("Build your own platform,
  exclusive for your country") under the **Titleman** brand. *"Three hours of work in ten minutes."*

## Their agent hook (this is the important part)
They lead with **MONEY + TRUST**, not tools:
- Earn **up to 90% of developer commission**; platform **100% free**
- **Keep your clients** — "permanently assigned to you"
- **Reliable payouts** — 1–3 days, **100% on time**
- **Confidential** — client buys direct, unaware of the platform (agent's brand up front)
- Then the tools: 1M+ listings analyzed → top 1%, 1-click PDF proposals, interactive Emirates map, analytics

## Their AI (forward-looking — a gap for us)
Forecasts **ROI, property value, investment risk**; **price forecast 1–5y**; **3-yr ROI**; **rental yield**;
**occupancy/booking status**; **market demand (8-week sales volume)**; **developer reliability rating**.
5 modules: **AI Search · AI Analytics · AI CRM · AI Calls · AI Messaging**.
Tools: New Development Map, Investment Insights Chat, "Real Estate AI Expert" (AI project summaries), Secondary Market Map.

## Proof / numbers they cite
200+ transactions · $5M+ paid to partners · 1M+ listings · 95% find it easy · 100% partner retention ·
100% on-time payouts · 100+ media mentions · deal examples $1.95M–$9.83M, partner rewards $65K–$297K.

## CTAs
"Get Your Platform – Sign Up Free!" · WhatsApp "Start chat right now" · "Book a free Demo session."

## So what — AgentConnect vs Realiste
| Area | Realiste | AgentConnect today | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tools (market intel, CRM, proposals, maps, AI chat, multi-market) | ✅ | ✅ (we match or beat) | hold |
| **Money/earnings hook** (90% commission, keep clients, fast payouts) | ✅ leads with it | ⚠️ we dropped it in the reposition | **add back, prominently** |
| **Forward-looking AI** (price forecast, liquidity, developer reliability score) | ✅ | ⚠️ our study is current-state | **add forecast fields** |
| **Trust stats / deal proof** | ✅ heavy | partial | **add a stats band + deal examples** |
| Truly global (4 live markets, study engine) | UAE-first | ✅ **we're more multi-market** | lean into it |
| White-label per agent / per country | ✅ (Plus / Titleman) | ✅ Plus tier | emphasize |

**Biggest takeaway:** Realiste wins agents with **money + trust first, tools second.** AgentConnect
currently sells tools first. We should put the **earnings promise** front-and-centre and add
**predictive AI** (price/liquidity/developer-reliability) to the Market Study — then we beat them on
breadth (4 markets + deeper study) *and* on the money message.
